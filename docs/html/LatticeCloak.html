LatticeCloaktyped placeholders and truthful generalization lattices for closed-box LLM inference

research report · 2026-07-02
Abstract

LatticeCloak anonymizes prompts for closed-box LLM inference: typed placeholders replace direct identifiers, truthful generalizations from per-span lattices replace quasi-identifiers, and a client-side substitution record lets rules invert the remote answer. Its per-token noise predecessor tied identity removal, premise preservation, and recovery to one scalar budget; LatticeCloak separates them. Direct-identifier detection reached 0.998 observed recall on the Text Anonymization Benchmark, and 97.8% of detected SynthPAI spans routed to zero-cost sources, leaving a 36-entity teacher residue. The per-span threshold τ shifted lattice levels (26→34 accepted at most-specific), yet aggregate surface probes stayed flat (lexical overlap 0.989, utility 0.971–0.972) and only a candidate-sensitive masked-LM attack responded, so pricing the knob requires an adversarial attacker: that evaluation, and the privacy-utility Pareto, are the explicitly pending next stage; round-trip evidence is smoke-scale.

§01

Introduction

problem · threat/setup · headline result

LatticeCloak is the successor to the abandoned InferDPT/RANTEXT line: instead of per-token ε-LDP swaps, it replaces direct identifiers with typed placeholders and quasi-identifiers with truthful generalizations drawn from a per-span lattice, keeping a client-side substitution record R that a rule-based extractor inverts on the way back. On the TAB benchmark the detection stack reaches 0.998 observed direct-identifier recall (the gate for the placeholder path), 97.5–97.8% of detected spans on SynthPAI route to zero-cost sources (rules, GeoNames, WordNet), and the full round trip runs end to end, with constructed prompts showing facts restored wherever the remote answer cites a recoverable substituted span.

The setting is a privacy layer for closed-box inference: a local client rewrites a prompt doc_orig into an anonymized doc_p, sends it to an untrusted remote LLM, receives out_p, and locally maps it back to out_final. The predecessor report (InferDPT & RANTEXT) established why the per-token mechanism fails: one scalar ε jointly governs identity removal, premise preservation, and extraction signal, and the end-to-end utility of that pipeline was produced by the extractor seeing the raw document, not by the perturbation. LatticeCloak decomposes the knob: placeholders carry the identifier load exactly and reversibly, lattices carry the premise load truthfully at an adjustable coarseness, and a masked-token probe (τ) sets the operating point per span rather than globally.

This page documents the prototype design and the measurements taken so far: the detection gate on TAB, span routing and teacher-cascade economics on SynthPAI, and a smoke-scale round trip with rule-only extraction. The adversarial evaluation (frontier-LLM attribute inference at matched realized privacy against the InferDPT baseline) is the next stage and is not claimed here.

§03

Method

design choices · roles · pipeline stages

Design choices. Three commitments shape the system. First, no autoregressive LLM runs in the deployed local path: the substitutor decomposes into detection, lookup, ranking, and inversion, each served by encoders or dictionaries (the whole stack is under 1B parameters and batches on an iGPU). Second, substitution is deterministic and auditable: replacements come from rule buckets, geographic and lexical hierarchies, or a cached teacher lattice, never from proximity sampling in an embedding space, which the predecessor page showed either preserves the referent or destroys the premise. Third, every failure is designed to fall toward abstention: an unresolvable span becomes a coarse type label (utility cost) rather than a leak (privacy cost).

Detection. GLiNER takes TAB's eight entity categories as free-text zero-shot labels, the only surveyed detector covering DEM, QUANTITY and CODE at all; Presidio contributes pattern recognizers (reference codes, money including spelled-out amounts, contact data). Spans merge widest-wins; pure-symbol spans and bare pronouns are dropped; lowercase "person" hits (occupations, roles) are retyped as demographic attributes so they generalize instead of becoming placeholders. Alias chains give every name variant one chain id, so "Sarah Johnson" and "Sarah" share one placeholder.

Lattice building. Dates and quantities bucket by rule ("March 3, 2021" → "March 2021" → "the early 2020s"; exact values never survive into any level). Places walk the GeoNames hierarchy; common nouns walk WordNet hypernyms. Only the residue, named entities no offline source covers, reaches the teacher cascade: E4B writes three candidate levels from the mention in context; every level must pass the NLI gate (with degenerate-substitution and self-reference rejections); failures regenerate once through Qwen3.6 with a chain-of-thought prompt; double failures floor to the type label. Results cache to disk keyed by entity and sense context, so runtime substitution is a dictionary lookup.

The cascade itself is the one component with internal moving parts:

FIG · M2 — teacher cascade with the NLI truthfulness gate (all local, offline, cached) measured on 36 entities: 31 settled at E4B · 4 escalated · 1 floored

Substitution. Direct identifiers become numbered typed placeholders per chain (<PERSON_1>, <CODE_2>). Each quasi identifier walks its lattice most specific first and accepts the first level whose MTI guess-back risk is below τ; candidates carrying the original's numbers or proper names are filtered before the walk. The output is doc_p plus R.

How the τ-walk chooses, and what it cannot see. Every choice is a fixed local heuristic. When the substitutor hits a quasi-identifier like "Oslo" it has a ladder of candidate replacements — the lattice, most specific first:

levelreplacement
0Oslo (original, most specific)
1a Norwegian city
2a Scandinavian capital
3a European city (coarsest)

The walk starts at level 0, runs the cheap local MTI probe ("if I mask this span, does an MLM guess 'Oslo' back?"), and takes the first level whose guess-back risk drops below the threshold τ. This decision is made per span, in isolation, using only the probe — it never sees what happens downstream: whether the remote LLM's answer survived the coarsening, whether the extractor could still map the output back, or whether the combination of choices across spans re-identifies the person anyway. That myopia is the operating assumption this prototype tests and the limitation the co-design next stage is meant to address.

Extraction. Rung A inverts R without any model: placeholders swap back exactly; generalization mentions in out_p are located by word-boundary exact match, then by fuzzy alignment snapped to word boundaries, and replaced with the original surface. A learned rung B (flan-t5-base LoRA over synthesized inversions) is specified but deliberately unbuilt until the measured rung A residue justifies it; the gap between the two is the prototype's central hypothesis test.

§05

Results

by pipeline stage

§5.1 Detection gate on TAB

All 127 TAB test documents, gold = union of all annotators' DIRECT/QUASI mentions (the strictest recall target), any-character-overlap matching, 44 s wall time. Aggregate: DIRECT any-recall 0.998 (typed 0.980, n=407), entity-level DIRECT recall 0.997, QUASI any-recall 0.857 (typed 0.783, n=6,524), precision proxy 0.716. The gate rule (DIRECT any-recall ≥ 0.95) passes, so the placeholder path does not need gold-span fallback.

FIG · 01 — TAB recall per entity type, direct vs quasisource: results/latticecloak_detection_gate.json · N=127 docs
Readviolet = DIRECT, green = QUASI. Direct identifiers are near-ceiling everywhere; the quasi weaknesses concentrate in DEM, MISC and QUANTITY, the categories needing document-level reasoning rather than span matching.

Conclusion. The detection gate passes (0.998 any-overlap, 0.997 entity-level, both observed on this test set). The measured weak categories (QUASI DEM 0.56, MISC 0.21, QUANTITY 0.25 any-recall) are the fuzzy "demographic attribute / other identifying event" spans; on the round-trip corpus their impact is priced by the attacker, not hidden by the detector. One smoke-run fix mattered: every initially missed DIRECT mention was an ECtHR application number, eliminated by a single numeric-reference pattern rather than threshold tuning.

§5.2 Span routing and teacher economics on SynthPAI

Routing of detected spans across lattice sources on the full 300-profile corpus (2,438 spans, post noise-cleanup: pure-symbol spans, bare pronouns and anchor-free temporals excluded). The teacher cascade serves 2.2% of spans (54 mentions, 36 unique entities); everything else is rules, GeoNames, WordNet, or placeholders. Full-corpus detection takes 49 s; the cascade settled 31 of 36 entities at E4B, escalated 4 to Qwen3.6, floored 1. The 60-document working slice used elsewhere on this page routes the same way (teacher share 2.5%, same rank order of sources), so the slice is representative of the corpus for routing purposes.

FIG · 02 — route shares of detected spans, full corpussource: results/latticecloak_inventory.json · N=2,438 spans / 300 docs
Readgray bars = share of detected spans per lattice source. The generative teacher is a 2.2% residue: substitution is almost entirely dictionaries and rules.

Conclusion. The zero-call bootstrap thesis holds at corpus scale: 97.8% of detected spans never touch a generative model, the full-corpus lattice cache builds in under two minutes on-box, and E4B handled most of the pilot's lattice writing (4/36 escalations, 1 floor). Scaling the substitutor is not a cost concern; the metered cost lives entirely in the evaluation round trips.

§5.3 The τ knob: leakage and utility probes across operating points

LatticeCloak has no privacy budget ε; its operating-point knob is τ, the guess-back threshold. Lowering τ demands lower contextual inferability before a lattice level is accepted, pushing quasi spans toward coarser levels and type-label floors; raising it admits more specific levels. Unlike ε it acts per span, not globally. To read LatticeCloak on the same instruments as the InferDPT report, the same probe families were computed over a τ sweep {0.005, 0.02, 0.1, 0.5} on the smoke slice (8 docs, 32 task answers per point). Leakage: overlap (the fraction of doc_orig's content words surviving verbatim into doc_p), PII leak (fuzzy survival of probe-detected PII spans), and MLM guess-back, the span analog of their inversion@10: the attacker reads doc_p with the replacement visible and a masked-LM guesses the original slot (top-50 hit rate; candidate-sensitive, so specific levels help the attacker). Utility: utility_control (SimCSE of out_final against the no-privacy control answer), utility_p_control (same for the raw out_p), coherence(gen_p) (SimCSE of out_p against its own prompt doc_p), and coherence(doc_p) (SimCSE of doc_p against doc_orig). Corpora and tasks differ between the two reports, so scales are comparable per instrument, not across pages.

FIG · 03 — τ sweep: leakage, utility, and mechanismsource: results/latticecloak_tau_sweep.json · N=8 docs, 36 generalized spans
ReadTop left (leakage, lower = more private): overlap and PII survival are flat; MLM guess-back, the span analog of inversion@10, is the only instrument that responds, rising to 0.21 at the loosest τ. Top right (utility, higher = more faithful): utility_control and utility_p_control coincide (inversion unexercised); coherence(doc_p) stays near 0.99. Bottom (mechanism, τ bars light→dark): the knob repositions the lattice, most-specific 26→34, floors 13→5.

Conclusion. τ demonstrably moves the substitution operating point (mechanism panel), but the aggregate surface instruments barely register it: overlap is 0.989 everywhere (span-targeted substitution leaves the document's lexical surface intact by design, unlike per-token perturbation, for which overlap is the natural instrument), coherence(doc_p) is 0.994 everywhere (the perturbed document stays semantically faithful), utility_control is 0.971–0.972 with out_final = out_p (inversion unexercised on these tasks), and PII leak is 0.842 at every τ. Inspection shows that survival number is dominated by the probe's own detector counting deliberately kept generic temporals ("daily", "Sundays") and misdetections ("Nah", "USB"), though it also catches one genuine miss class: an education phrase ("Sci Master's") survives, consistent with the DEM weakness measured on TAB. The one instrument that responds is the candidate-sensitive one: MLM guess-back rises from 0.18 to 0.21 at the loosest τ, directionally as designed, though within noise for 36 spans. The methodological consequence is the predecessor taxonomy's F4 lesson landing on this method too: aggregate surface probes cannot price LatticeCloak's knob; attack-shaped instruments respond, and the full adversarial evaluation is what the next stage runs.

§5.4 Round trip and rule-only extraction

Smoke scale: 8 SynthPAI documents × (1 summarization + up to 3 teacher-generated QA) = 32 task tuples through the remote model, then rung A inversion. Inversion statistics over the 32 answers: 0 placeholders to swap (first-person corpus, no named persons in the slice), 0 exact and 0 fuzzy narrowings, 128 substituted spans never mentioned in the answers. On these task types the remote answers paraphrase around the substituted details, so rule extraction has little to do and the outputs are already coherent. The behavior under answers that do cite substituted facts is shown by four constructed prompts (fact-restating task, same remote model), with the no-privacy control for comparison:

Conclusion. The remote model reasons validly on the coarsened premises (it even correctly reports "occupation not specified" where the generalization removed it, rather than hallucinating). Rung A restores every fact the answer cites via a recoverable phrase (placeholders, "a financial institution" → Goldman Sachs, "a city in Illinois" → Chicago, "a medical building" → Mercy Hospital). The residue against the control decomposes into three classes: precision the substitution deliberately suppressed (a salary range instead of $200,000), narrowing misses when the remote rephrases or reformats the mention ("a Massachusetts city" for "a city in Massachusetts", currency-symbol drift), and grammar seams after narrowing ("a 40 data scientist"). The latter two are the training target for rung B; the first is the privacy price the Pareto will put a number on.

§06

Findings

claims · grounds · scope

1 · On TAB, the placeholder path achieves near-complete observed DIRECT recall (Partial). Any-overlap recall 0.998, entity-level 0.997, typed 0.980 (n=407 mentions, 127 docs). This is a benchmark detection result, not a privacy guarantee: any-overlap matching is lenient, and the numeric-reference pattern that eliminated the miss class was added after inspecting failures on five documents from the same test set, so the headline number is not a frozen-detector hold-out. Grounds: §5.1. Scope: union-of-annotators gold; legal-domain English; GLiNER small + Presidio.

2 · TAB quasi-identifier detection has measured recall weaknesses in DEM, MISC and QUANTITY (Supported, negative finding). QUASI any-recall: DEM 0.56, MISC 0.21, QUANTITY 0.25 against 0.86 overall, with substantial denominators (n=474/411/287). Grounds: §5.1. Scope: these categories require document-level inference, and misses pass into doc_p unprotected; the planned attacker evaluation prices this, the detector number alone does not.

3 · On SynthPAI, detected-span substitution is dominated by dictionary and rule routes (Partial). 97.5–97.8% of detected spans route to zero-cost sources; the generative teacher covers 36 unique entities across 300 profiles, built once in under two minutes on-box. Route counts do not measure substitution quality, and the denominator is detected spans on one informal-register corpus; the general form of the claim needs multi-domain replication and a substitution-correctness audit. Grounds: §5.2.

4 · In a 36-entity pilot, a 4B-class local teacher handled most lattice writing when paired with a verification cascade (Partial). 31/36 settled at E4B; 4 escalations to Qwen3.6, 1 type-label floor; the NLI gate plus degeneracy and self-reference rejections caught the failure modes observed in this pilot (wrong-sense hypernyms, vacuous entailments, self-referential candidates). Adequacy in general is not established: n=36, one corpus, no blind accuracy measurement of accepted candidates, no gate false-accept rate. Grounds: §5.2, §3.

5 · In the 32-answer smoke test, summarization/QA outputs did not reuse substituted spans, so inversion went mostly unexercised (Partial). 128/128 substituted spans were absent from the answers; outputs were coherent without inversion. Constructed fact-restating prompts show rung A restoring the recoverable cited facts with three residue classes (§5.4). Whether answers "rarely" cite substituted spans in general, and whether rule-only extraction suffices, remains a hypothesis for the Pareto stage: 8 documents, one task suite, one remote model, no utility metric yet.

6 · In the 8-doc τ sweep, the knob mechanically changed lattice operating points, but the tested aggregate surface probes were effectively flat (Partial). Most-specific acceptances rose 26→34 of 36 spans and floors fell 13→5 across τ = 0.005–0.5, while overlap (0.989), PII leak (0.842), coherence(doc_p) (0.994) and utility_control (0.971–0.972) did not move; the one responsive instrument was the candidate-sensitive MLM guess-back (0.18→0.21 at the loosest τ, within noise for n=36). This suggests aggregate probes are too coarse for span-targeted substitution while attack-shaped ones respond, and motivates adversarial pricing in the next stage; it does not prove surface probes in general cannot be made sensitive. The PII-leak diagnosis (probe-detector noise plus one genuine education-phrase miss) rests on manual inspection of one document. Grounds: §5.3.

§07

Analysis

why each finding holds — the mechanism behind the numbers

Why direct recall is near ceiling while quasi lags. Direct identifiers are surface-regular (names, codes) and sit squarely in the detector's pattern and NER competence; the one systematic miss class (application numbers) was a regex away. DEM/MISC/QUANTITY spans are open-class and contextual ("a mother of three", "the incident that spring"): recognizing them is inference, not matching, which is the known boundary of span-level detection and the reason the evaluation design measures privacy as attacker outcome rather than detector recall.

Why the teacher residue is so small. SynthPAI's quasi-identifiers are dominated by occupations, education, locations, ages and incomes: common nouns, places and values, which WordNet, GeoNames and rule buckets already cover. Only branded or culturally specific entities (Netflix, IKEA, konbini) fall through, and their number grows sublinearly with corpus size because entities repeat.

Why the cascade's checks are load-bearing. Three observed failure classes never reached the cache: fluent wrong-sense hypernyms for rare foreign terms, vacuous entailments from degenerate substitutions in fragmentary contexts, and candidates containing the entity itself. Each was caught by a targeted rejection (case-insensitive substitution with fail-closed matching, repeated-word degeneracy check, self-reference filter) in front of the entailment score, not by the score alone. One probe property matters for interpretation: masking hides the candidate, so MTI risk measures whether the context gives the original away, and a span inferable at every lattice level correctly lands on the most general form with its risk recorded in R.

Why the surface probes are flat across τ. The sweep only re-chooses lattice levels for 36 generalized spans inside documents whose remaining thousands of content words are untouched: document-level lexical overlap is dominated by the untouched text, so it cannot register which level a span sits at. Per-token perturbation, which rewrites every token as ε moves, is the regime those probes were built for. The utility probe is similarly saturated because the remote answers paraphrase around the substituted details regardless of their coarseness on these tasks. The instruments are not wrong, they are aimed at a different mechanism; the operating point of a span-targeted method has to be priced where its effect lives, in what an adversary can infer. The MLM guess-back result is the small confirmation: the only probe that reads doc_p the way an attacker would (replacement visible, original guessed) is also the only one that moves with τ.

Why answers rarely cite substituted spans. Summaries and short QA answers compress: they mention what the question demands and paraphrase the rest. Substituted details are exactly the specifics such answers drop. The corollary cuts both ways: utility survives substitution largely without extraction on these tasks, and extraction value concentrates in tasks whose answers must restate the protected attributes, which is where the rung A residue classes (rephrase misses, grammar seams) become the binding constraint and rung B earns or fails to earn its keep.

§08

Discussion

what it buys · implications · limitations · next

Implications. The predecessor page located InferDPT's failure in one scalar ε doing three jobs; LatticeCloak's measurements so far are consistent with the decomposition: identifiers route into exact, reversible placeholders (0.998 observed benchmark recall), premises are handled truthfully at tunable coarseness (lattices, τ per span), and in the smoke evidence the extraction burden shrank to a rule pass wherever the task did not restate protected attributes. The remote model reasoning correctly on coarsened premises, including declining to invent removed facts, is the property token-level noise could not deliver.

Limitations. No adversarial privacy number yet: the frontier-LLM attribute-inference attacker and the utility metrics on out_final are the next stage, so nothing here is a privacy–utility claim at matched realized privacy. The round-trip evidence is smoke-scale (8 documents, 32 answers) plus four constructed prompts. Detection weakness on DEM/MISC/QUANTITY is measured on legal text and unmeasured implicit disclosures ("my 9-to-5 at the hospital") never enter any span denominator. Coreference is surface aliasing, adequate for first-person Reddit text and known-inadequate for nominal anaphora in legal documents. Sense ambiguity in offline lookups (Cambridge, UK vs MA) is accepted as a rule-based ceiling by design decision, deferred to the learned-substitutor comparison.

Next. The Pareto stage: τ sweep against the InferDPT ε sweep on identical documents and tasks, privacy scored by held-out frontier attackers on doc_p and out_final, utility scored against references and gold answers; then rung B trained on synthesized inversions to price the measured rung A residue. Kill criteria are pre-registered: if rung B does not beat rules at matched realized privacy, the finding is that R plus rules suffice.
