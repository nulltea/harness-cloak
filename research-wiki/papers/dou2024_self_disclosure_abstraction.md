# Reducing Privacy Risks in Online Self-Disclosures with Language Models

## One-line thesis
Introduces the **span abstraction** task — rewrite a self-disclosure span to a less specific but
still-informative form ("I'm 16F" → "I'm a teenage girl") — with abstractions generated by GPT-3.5/GPT-4
as teacher and **distilled into a local finetuned model**.

## Method
Two-part pipeline over user-generated (Reddit-style) text: (1) a finetuned detector identifies
self-disclosure spans across a taxonomy of disclosure categories; (2) an abstraction model rewrites the
span to a coarser phrasing preserving the communicative intent. Abstraction training data is produced by
prompting large teacher models, then distilled into a smaller local model — teacher knowledge lands in
*weights*, not in a lookup artifact.

## Key Results
- Established span abstraction as a task with human evaluation of abstraction quality; the distilled
  local abstractor approaches teacher quality (per the paper's evaluation).
- Used as the strongest truthfulness baseline in
  [INTACT](pilan2024_truthful_sanitization.md) ([arXiv 2412.12928](https://arxiv.org/abs/2412.12928))'s
  manual study: **95.2% truthful / 78.3% more abstract** (vs INTACT 93.2%/81.3%, Presidio 19.7%/36.2%).

## Relevance to This Project
**Why surfaced:** during D1 implementation research as the closest published prior to two D1 design
elements: (1) the **span-abstraction substitution family** (truthful coarsening of quasi-identifier
spans — the same surrogate family as INTACT's generalizations and D1's lattice walk), and (2) the
**teacher-generates → local-model-serves pattern**: D1's prototype shortcut precomputes per-entity
generalization *lattices* with a teacher LLM into a lookup cache, which is this paper's distillation
move with a dict in place of weights (cf. Symbolic Knowledge Distillation, West et al.,
[arXiv 2110.07178](https://arxiv.org/abs/2110.07178)). No published work was found doing the cached-
lattice variant for anonymization specifically. **Fit:** validates that abstraction quality survives
distillation to a small local model — the load-bearing assumption behind D1's residual infiller.
